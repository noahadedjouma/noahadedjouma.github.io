What is a Transformers?

That would take about 2.5 million years to reach. People definitely won’t be seeing Cybertron for a long time.Transformers to put it simply are alien robots that can transform. What's so unique about Transformers that other robots don't have?


What is so unique about Transformers from other robots? What’s also unique about Transformers from other robots is that they can do insane task on their own. Story


Like other popular pop culture icons Transformers have a very intresting back story. Billions of years ago, Cybertron was a planet of peace...until the Decepticons, lusting for power, began a terrible war. Not designed for combat, the Autobots were overwhelmed and subjugated by their evil opponents. While many Autobots fled Cybertron, a few valiant survivors devised new tactics and launched a counter-offensive on their arch-foes. And thus began a terrible series of wars. Many times, both sides have claimed victory, but this has been short-lived, for the Autobots have overthrown Decepticon tyrants, and, likewise, Decepticon treachery has toppled many a peaceful Autobot ruler. And to this day, the war rages on.

Beginnings


Transformers started as a toy line in 1984 and soon turned into a show the same year called "Transformers Generation 1". The show was a gigantic hit rivaling the most popular show at the time "He-man". Transformers were bought by kids, teens and especially adults. Transformers were taken off the shelves left and right and had a comic book series written by Marvel comics. Hasbro the creator of Transformers were surprised at it's success so they made a season 2 for the show which was a massive hit. Then Transformers had their first movie "Transformers the movie". It was hit and the best animated movie of it's time. Years after the show ended they created new shows and had it's own live-action movie making billions of dollars.

Future


As Transformers grow in popularity people demand more and more products such as movies. Transformers movies had a bad rap in the beginning due to the fact it had a terrible director. The movies story were fast paced and convoluted but what it was terrible in was the CGI and action. In this deparment they did not hold back, having one of the greatest action scenes in cinema history. The CGI in the movies are absolutely stunning making the Transformers as realsitic as possible. In the future the Transformers will be getting a new rebooted live-action series directed by the well respected Travis Knight and hopefully many more movies to come.
